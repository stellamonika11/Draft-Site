UPLOAD

Send your or someone you knows art to the email adress below, it is recommended that the age of the artist is between 1 and 13. The art can be old or new, and in any medium. Remember the art does not have to be conventionally “good” it just has to be art. Please agree to the term below before sending your artwork.

Send your artwork to stellas@ocadu.ca to be uploaded on the site.

1. Take a photo of the work in bright even lighting.

2. Make sure the work is in focus and fills the frame.

3. Email the photo to the email adresss above with the name, age and optional title of the work.

When you send your work you are consenting to having this artwork displayed on the website indefinitely.
